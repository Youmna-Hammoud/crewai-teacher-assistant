Lesson Topic: Introduction to Musical Notes

Notes:
- Musical notes are symbols representing the pitch and duration of a sound.
- The seven natural notes are: A, B, C, D, E, F, G.
- Notes can be sharp (#) or flat (â™­) to indicate pitch changes.
- A staff is a set of five lines where notes are written.
- The treble clef and bass clef indicate the pitch range of the notes.
- Whole notes, half notes, quarter notes, and eighth notes have different durations.
- Tempo tells how fast or slow the music should be played.

Key Vocabulary:
- Note
- Sharp
- Flat
- Staff
- Treble clef
- Bass clef
- Tempo
