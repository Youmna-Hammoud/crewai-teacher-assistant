Lesson Topic: Introduction to Fractions

Notes:
- A fraction represents a part of a whole.
- A fraction has two parts: numerator (top) and denominator (bottom).
- Example: 1/2 means one part out of two equal parts.
- Proper fractions: numerator < denominator.
- Improper fractions: numerator > denominator.
- Mixed numbers combine a whole number and a fraction.
- Adding fractions with the same denominator: add numerators, keep denominator.
- Converting improper fractions to mixed numbers: divide numerator by denominator.

Key Vocabulary:
- Numerator
- Denominator
- Proper fraction
- Improper fraction
- Mixed number
